FUNCTIONALITY


Through this website, users can register and subsequently login to place orders from nearby Vendors. Vendor manager can get their business registered on the website. After approval from the admin, the Vendor will be added to the list of nearby homemade shops and will be visible to the users. The vendor manager can edit their menu (change price, add/delete items ) at any time. Vendors or Local Shops can also accept/reject orders.
